About XCONNECT

XCONNECT.to is your destination for accessing free live television streams from across the globe. We're dedicated to making it simple and enjoyable to discover and watch international channels. Browse an extensive selection of programming, from worldwide news broadcasts and live sporting events to films, entertainment programming, and cultural content. Experience global cultures through our easy-to-use platform: select a nation on our 3D interactive globe, navigate via the convenient sidebar, or click the "Random Channel" feature for spontaneous discovery. You never know what fascinating content awaits! XCONNECT.to delivers a quick, straightforward method to access live television channels internationally—no registration required, no complicated processes—simply click and start watching.

Our Purpose

XCONNECT.to prioritizes simplicity, dependability, and smooth viewing experiences. We're dedicated to connecting you with compelling worldwide content while eliminating clutter and dead links. By concentrating on functional channels, we save you from buffering issues and disappointment—allowing you to simply tune in and watch.

Political Neutrality Statement

XCONNECT.to maintains strict political neutrality. We provide straightforward, accessible exploration of free IPTV and YouTube channels globally, without endorsing any political or territorial positions.

For consistency, we adhere to United Nations (UN) country classifications. We aim to present channels according to their official operating country while maintaining a neutral, welcoming platform for international audiences.

Legal Disclaimer

XCONNECT.to offers access to publicly available streams without hosting, owning, or managing any video content. We provide convenient exploration and viewing of streams that we reasonably believe have been authorized for public access by rights holders. Our approach follows the standards set by the IPTV-org GitHub repository, a community-maintained IPTV link collection. XCONNECT.to neither hosts nor stores video content.

Should you believe a stream violates your copyright, you have these options:

Submit a DMCA takedown request to IPTV-org, since they manage the repository providing these links.

Contact feedback@XCONNECT.to directly. We'll quickly assess your notification and take suitable action, including link removal from our site when warranted. XCONNECT.to honors valid DMCA takedown requests

Please note: Removing links from XCONNECT.to doesn't eliminate content from the internet. For infringing content, we recommend also contacting the stream's hosting provider.

While XCONNECT.to endeavors to link exclusively to authorized streams, we cannot verify the copyright status of every link. XCONNECT.to may remove content links at its discretion without advance warning.

Using XCONNECT.to indicates your acceptance of these terms.
